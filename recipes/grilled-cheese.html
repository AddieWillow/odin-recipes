Grilled Cheese

Description

A delicious and simple staple, this dish is the definition of comfort food.

It takes no skill, little prep, and only a handful of ingredients to make a tasty grilled cheese.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Butter
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet for 5 minutes and melt butter.
  2. Slice the cheese into thin or thick slices, as preferred.
  3. Spread a thin layer or mayo on each side of the bread.
  4. Place cheese between slices and set sandwich on skillet.
  5. Cook until the butter sizzles and the bread is golden brown.
  6. Flip the sandwich over and continue cooking until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
