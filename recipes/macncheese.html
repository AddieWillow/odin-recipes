Mac 'n Cheese

Description

Mac 'n cheese is a gooey, savory dish that satisfies nearly everyone's taste for cheesy goodness.

You can make mac 'n cheese in a variety of styles, this is just my own favorite!

Ingredients

  • Cheddar cheese, shredded
  • Elbow macaroni
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil salted water and cook the macaroni al dente.
  2. Drain the pasta.
  3. As the pasta drains, melt the butter in the saucepan.
  4. Add shredded cheese and milk, slowly and stirring constantly.
  5. Add the pasta back into the pan and stir vigorously.
  6. Season with pepper and serve.
