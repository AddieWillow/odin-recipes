Cheese Quesadilla

Description

Quesadillas are a simple way to fix an empty stomach. Best of all, you can build on a simple cheese quesadilla and make it so much more!

Get started with these easy steps and build your own quesadilla.

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • Shredded cheese, any kind
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat a pan for 5 minutes
  2. Spread the shredded cheese on half of the tortilla.
  3. Add any extra ingredients before folding the empty half of the tortilla over the cheesy half.
  4. Melt a small amount of butter in the pan.
  5. Set the quesadilla in the pan and cook until golden brown on both sides, flipping frequently with a spatula.
